About

Currently working as a Software Development Engineer at Amadeus, Working mainly with C++, for the Fare and pricing engine.
Amadeus Fares & Pricing Engine is a powerful search and calculation tool, which processes large volumes of fares, returning accurate pricing in real time, even for complex international itineraries.

The Amadeus Fares & Pricing Engine uses the latest processing technology and unique proprietary algorithms to deliver reliable fares and pricing for the 30 million public and private fare transactions performed each day. This pricing tool is currently used by more than 150 airlines, 70,000 travel agencies and 200 markets globally and is fully integrated into Altéa Reservation.
